Manifesto

We build tools
that respect
your attention.

— Less interface. More clarity.

— Your space. Your rules.

— Tools that disappear when you need them to.

— Free today. Free tomorrow. Always.

Khame Space began as a simple idea — that the software people use every day should feel calm, not chaotic. That productivity tools don't need to be feature-packed to be powerful.

Every app is built by one person, with intention. There are no investors. No roadmaps written by committees. No dark patterns. Just tools that try to be genuinely useful.

  • 01Minimal surface. No feature exists unless it earns its place. We remove before we add.
  • 02Private by default. Your data is yours. Nothing leaves your machine unless you explicitly decide.
  • 03Free, always. Good tools should not require a subscription. Funded by belief, not paywalls.
  • 04Always improving. Imperfect and honest — every app will grow until it becomes what it needs to be.

A note from the developer



These apps are built around the idea of an ecosystem of minimalist tools. Some are imperfect and will continue to grow until each one becomes exactly what it needs to be.

Thank you for being here early. Thank you for trying something that is still finding its shape. Thank you for supporting me.
