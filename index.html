Manifesto

We build tools
that respect
your attention.

Less interface. More clarity.

Your space. Your rules.

Tools that disappear when you need them to.

Free today. Free tomorrow. Always.

Khame Space began as a simple idea — that the software people use every day should feel calm, not chaotic. That productivity tools don't need to be feature-packed to be powerful. That minimalism is not about removing value, it's about keeping only what matters.

Every app in this ecosystem is built by one person, with intention. There are no investors. No roadmaps written by committees. No dark patterns. Just tools that try to be genuinely useful to people who care about how they work.

The name says it all. Khame Space is your space. A private, quiet corner of the digital world where the tools serve you — not the other way around.

  • 01Minimal surface. No feature exists unless it earns its place. We remove before we add.
  • 02Private by default. Your data is yours. Nothing leaves your machine unless you explicitly decide.
  • 03Free, always. Good tools should not require a subscription. Khame Space is funded by belief, not paywalls.
  • 04Always improving. Imperfect and honest — every app will grow until it is exactly what it needs to be.

A note from the developer

These apps are made off of the thought of an ecosystem of minimalist tools. Some are imperfect and will continue to improve and grow till each one becomes exactly what it needs to be. Every update, every new app, every improvement is a step toward that vision.

Thank you for being here early. Thank you for trying something that is still finding its shape. Thank you for supporting me.
